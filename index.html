About me

Hey there, thank you for stumbling upon my details page, I don't know how to advertise this so you did half the battle by finding it for me! I don't have much to say to start out with, I guess the first interesting thing to note is that I've modeled before, that's me on the catwalk to the left. It's super fun to be up there actually, you only have about 30 seconds of spotlight but it's enough to make the hours of waiting for the night worth it. I don't do much of it anymore thanks to current events but it's for sure a unique experience that everyone should try out if they can!

Besides being tall and shapely enough to present clothes with, most of my hobbies are pretty nerdy. I've been playing PC games for about a decade now, a hobby that I took after from my dad, who sadly does not spend too much time with the craft anymore. Asides from recreation, computers are also a deep interest in my life, my current major is in Cybersecurity and I've spent a good amount of personal time already studying and learning about the trade before entering UNO. Before I went to University. With all the time I spend wired to a computer though, I try my best to avoid as much social media as I can, which is probably the secret to having a healthy relationship with technology after spending so many thousands hours with it by now.

A pretty cool fact that's relevant to the class actually is that I've spent a couple years in/out of highschool as a website developer downtown! I had a pretty cool internship opportunity at a web dev company that turned into a part-time job, so I'm not exactly new to web development. I've already dealt with back-end programming with dynamic websites in asp.net, so I can't wait to get into some of the more technical asepcts of the class.
